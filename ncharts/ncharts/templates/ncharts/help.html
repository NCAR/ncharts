Bugs and Idiosyncracies

  • Response will be slow if you ask for a large quantity of data. Data reduction and compression are not supported.
  • Plots do not appear on the stock Android browser. Use Chrome
  • Time axis labels are in UTC.
  • On phones, if the plot occupies the entire screen you might not be able to scroll around.

Other Help

  • For a time-series trace, click on the variable name at the bottom of a plot to toggle the visibility of the trace.
